Obscuring dust lanes, young blue star clusters and reddish star forming regions surround a core of yellowish light from an older population of stars. The sharp image data also reveal more distant background galaxies seen right through NGC 2442&#39;s star clusters and nebulae. The image spans about 75,000 light-years at the estimated distance of NGC 2442.</p>
